Our Story

Culinary Excellence Since 1995

Founded by Chef Antoine Moreau, GPS Highway Restaurant has been serving exceptional Indian cuisine with a modern twist for over two decades. Our restaurant is a celebration of culinary artistry, where tradition meets innovation.

We source only the finest ingredients from local farmers and producers who share our commitment to quality and sustainability. Each dish is a masterpiece, carefully crafted to delight your senses.

Our elegant dining room provides the perfect setting for an unforgettable dining experience, whether you're celebrating a special occasion or simply enjoying an evening of exceptional food and wine.
