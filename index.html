Our Story

Serving Authentic Flavors Since 2010

GPS Highway Restaurant was founded with a simple mission: to bring the authentic taste of India to the highways of Maharashtra. Our chefs use traditional recipes passed down through generations, combined with the freshest local ingredients.

What started as a small roadside eatery has grown into a beloved destination for travelers and locals alike, known for our flavorful curries, tandoori specialties, and warm hospitality.

We take pride in offering a dining experience that feels like home, with every dish prepared with care and served with a smile. Our commitment to quality and authenticity has made us a landmark on the Nagpur-Jabalpur highway.
